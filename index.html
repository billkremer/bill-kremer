About

Bill is a Full-Stack Software Engineer, specializing in the SEAN stack (PostgreSQL, Express, Angular, Node) and Front-End Logic.

At Donaldson Company, Bill created easy-to-use safety program sites, including program resources and computer-based-training. This allowed sites to reduce classroom instruction and focus on hands-on coaching.

The work on these sites inspired him to pursue a career in Software Engineering, to develop easy-to-use and intuitive web applications that users are excited to use again and again.

As a Safety Engineer, Bill routinely met with stakeholders from the front office to production employees, with manufacturing engineers, and also executive management. Addressing the needs of these very different roles takes patience and understanding.

Baba Baby Boom

Busy parents often have very little time to think about maintaining a baby book and sending updates to grandparents.

Baba Baby Boom allows parents to maintain and update a log of baby’s achievements and recent photos, and sends a simple email to the parent’s contact list sharing those recent achievements and photos.

  • Site: https://baba-baby-boom.herokuapp.com
  • Date: March 2017
  • GitHub: GitHub Repository

  • Technology Used:
  • PostgreSQL
  • Express
  • AngularJS
  • Node.js
  • Nodemailer
  • Amazon Web Services S3
  • Bootstrap
  • Heroku

Angel Foundation

Gathering statistical data for grant applications and reports required Angel Foundation staff to wait until the database administrator was able to retrieve the information.

This report-generating web application allows Angel Foundation staff to quickly and easily obtain the information they need to complete the work for their customers.

The application also allows the staff to save their frequently used data searches for use later. Data visualizations such as charts and graphs help create dramatic presentations.

  • Client: Angel Foundation
  • Date: April 2017

  • Technology Used:
  • PostgreSQL
  • Express
  • AngularJS
  • Node.js
  • D3.js
  • Bootstrap
  • Alertify
  • Heroku
